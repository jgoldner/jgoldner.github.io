Our Story

Farming is one of the most fundamental and necessary of all services,
and eating is one of the most fundamental and necessary of all human rights.

Food for Hudson provides 20 weeks of farm fresh vegetables to 55 families on a sliding scale basis. The produce is grown with love and care at Lineage Farm using sustainable and regenerative practices and is distributed weekly. Access to affordable, fresh produce is a long standing issue in Hudson and this program seeks to address the disconnect between the bounty grown in Columbia County, and the critical issue of fresh, affordable food access for all low income families.

Support Us

The city of Hudson sits in the center of what was once known as the "bread basket" of New York, and Columbia County alone produces millions of pounds of fruits and vegetables every year. However, in the midst of all this abundance we see rising rates of diet related illnesses, like diabetes and obesity. According to the USDA, the rate of obesity among low-income preschoolers in Hudson is higher than the state average.

 

When you support Good Food for Hudson you help dismantle the cost and access barriers between these families and local, healthy vegetables. You are also supporting local farmers, a key to sustaining a thriving local economy in Columbia County.
